Join. The. Mayhem.

We are the Instruments of Mayhem, a theatrical bellydance troupe based in Hamilton. With over twelve years of experience teaching and performing both classical bellydance and our own unique style of the art form, we are expert teachers and performers. Whether you're after a fun, energetic workout, looking to learn and eventually perform bellydance in earnest or simply want to meet new people in a sociable, active environment, our classes are a great way to achieve your goals and have a blast doing it. Come to one of our free introductory lessons and find out just how much fun it is to join the mayhem!

Our Teachers

Eloise Harrop

Eloise is the IoM’s principal teacher. She has been practicing classical Egyptian bellydance for over twelve years, and has also trained in tribal/tribal inspired bellydance, burlesque and cabaret performance and contact dance. With this background, experience and training, she and Anna have created theatrical bellydance, a new and unique style that uses dance as a storytelling tool to bring to life a world of wonder, mystery, fun, empowerment and positivity.

She has three rules:

  1. Don’t be a dick
  2. Don’t compare yourself to anyone else: you are unique
  3. Have fun. This is an environment to grow. Learn and enjoy

Anna Cruse

Anna is a performer and part-time teacher with the IoM. With twenty years on and off performing classical Egyptian bellydance and training in multiple other forms of dance, she brings skill, energy and enthusiasm to the stage. An inveterate traveler and explorer, she takes particular pleasure in introducing bellydance to new people, and runs many of the IoM’s crash courses, particularly the skills section of the Belly Laughs classes.

Beginning Bellydance

No experience with bellydance, but keen to learn? Want to build confidence in your body and move fluidly and fluently? Our Beginning Bellydance class teaches you the basic skills and movements needed for bellydance and builds into a basic choreography, building a strong foundation for further learning in bellydance and developing the co-ordination and core control needed for many other forms of dance.

Location: The Studio Hamilton, 90 Commerce Street, Frankton

Time: 12 PM Saturday

Prices

First Lesson: Free

Casual students: $25/lesson ($20 with Student ID or Community Services Card)

Block course students: $190/ten week course ($150 with Student ID or Community Services Card)
