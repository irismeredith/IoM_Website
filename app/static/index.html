We are the Instruments of Mayhem, a theatrical bellydance troupe based in Hamilton. We break the boundaries of what you thought was bellydance, bringing over thirty years of total experience and classical training to bear on creating an expressive, dramatic and mysterious form of the art. Has this gotten you curious? Good. Come to a class and find out just how much fun it is to join the mayhem!

Our Teachers

Eloise Harrop

Eloise is the IoM’s principal teacher. She has been practicing classical Egyptian bellydance for over twelve years, and has also trained in tribal/tribal inspired bellydance, burlesque and cabaret performance and contact dance. With this background, experience and training, she and Anna have created theatrical bellydance, a new and unique style that uses dance as a storytelling tool to bring to life a world of wonder, mystery, fun, empowerment and positivity.

She has three rules:

  1. Don’t be a dick
  2. Don’t compare yourself to anyone else: you are unique
  3. Have fun. This is an environment to grow. Learn and enjoy

Anna Cruse

Anna is a performer and part-time teacher with the IoM. With twenty years on and off performing classical Egyptian bellydance and training in multiple other forms of dance, she brings skill, energy and enthusiasm to the stage. An inveterate traveler and explorer, she takes particular pleasure in introducing bellydance to new people, and runs many of the IoM’s crash courses, particularly the skills section of the Belly Laughs classes.

Beginning Bellydance

Seen us tearing up the stage and want to take the first step on the path to doing it yourself? Want to push the boundaries of what you can do with your body? Want to learn to move fluidly, fluently and mysteriously? Our Beginning Bellydance class teaches you the basic skills and movements needed for bellydance and builds into a basic choreography, building a strong foundation for further learning in bellydance and developing the co-ordination and core control needed for many other forms of dance.

Location: The Studio Hamilton, 90 Commerce Street, Frankton

Time: 12 PM Saturday

Prices

Sick of the pink and frilly workouts that are all the rage these days? Belly Laughs

Belly Laughs is a two hour introductory bellydance class located at venues around the upper North Island. It’s a great opportunity to meet new people, have fun and pick up new skills, and is perfect for events, groups and organisations.
